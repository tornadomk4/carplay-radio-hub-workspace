# Customer Auto-Response Knowledge Base

## Common Questions & Approved Responses

### Fitment Questions

**Q: Will this fit my [year] [make] [model]?**
A: Our radios are made for standard double DIN radio openings. Enter your year, make, and model at [carplayradiohub.com](https://carplayradiohub.com) to check fitment before ordering. Most standard double DIN vehicles are supported.

**Q: Do I need a dash kit / bezel?**
A: Most vehicles need a vehicle-specific bezel or dash kit. The order form collects your vehicle info so the correct parts can be matched to your kit. We include the vehicle-specific bezel and wiring harness adapter with each order.

**Q: Is it plug and play?**
A: Our radios are designed for standard double DIN openings and include a vehicle-specific wiring harness adapter for a cleaner install. Some vehicles may still need minor wiring or trim work. Many customers handle the install themselves or have a local audio shop do it.

### Pricing & Payment

**Q: How much is it?**
A: The radio package is $109.99 (sale price, regularly $150). It includes the 7-inch touchscreen radio, 12 LED backup camera, backup camera cable, remote control, wiring harness, mounting brackets, screws, and the vehicle-specific bezel and harness adapter.

**Q: Do you accept [PayPal/Venmo/CashApp]?**
A: We currently accept card payments through Stripe secure checkout. You can use any credit or debit card — no Stripe account needed.

**Q: Is there a warranty?**
A: We offer a 30-day replacement policy for defective units. If your radio arrives defective or doesn't work properly, contact us within 30 days and we'll make it right.

### Shipping

**Q: How fast does it ship?**
A: Orders are prepared after fitment details and payment are received. Timely orders are prepared for same-day shipping, with delivery expected in 4 days or less.

**Q: Do you ship to [state/country]?**
A: We ship within the US. Delivery timing depends on your location and carrier timing.

**Q: Where is it shipping from?**
A: Products are shipped directly to you. Delivery is typically 4 days or less after the order is processed.

### Product Questions

**Q: Does it have wireless CarPlay?**
A: Yes! Our radios support both wired and wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. You can connect wirelessly or use a cable — whatever you prefer.

**Q: Does it come with a backup camera?**
A: Yes, a 12 LED rear-view backup camera is included in every package.

**Q: What's the screen size?**
A: 7-inch touchscreen display.

**Q: Does it work with steering wheel controls?**
A: Our radios support iDatalink Maestro integration, which retains steering wheel controls, factory cameras, and vehicle settings in compatible vehicles.

**Q: Can I connect my phone wirelessly?**
A: Yes — wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto are supported, plus Bluetooth 5.1 for music streaming and hands-free calling.

### Installation

**Q: Is installation included?**
A: Installation is not included since products are shipped directly to you. Many customers handle the install themselves, or have a local audio shop install it. We can point you in the right direction if you need help finding an installer.

**Q: How hard is it to install?**
A: For a standard double DIN vehicle, it's a straightforward swap. We include the vehicle-specific harness and bezel to make it cleaner. If you're comfortable with basic car audio work, you can do it yourself. Otherwise, any car audio shop can handle it.

### Order Issues

**Q: I ordered but haven't received it yet.**
A: Check your email for the order confirmation and tracking number. If you don't have one, message us with your name and email used on the order and we'll look it up.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: If the order hasn't shipped yet, we can cancel it. Message us with your order details ASAP and we'll check the status.

**Q: Can I return it?**
A: We have a 30-day replacement policy for defective units. For other return situations, message us and we'll work it out.

## Tone Guidelines
- Friendly but professional
- Short answers (2-4 sentences max)
- Always include a link to the site or a way to continue the conversation
- Never mention Amazon/Prime
- Never overpromise on shipping speed or installation difficulty
- When in doubt, direct them to the fitment checker on the site
