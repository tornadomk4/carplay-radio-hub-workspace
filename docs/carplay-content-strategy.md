# CarPlay Radio Hub — Content Strategy

## Market Research Insights (2026-06-28)

### Trending CarPlay Features (2026)
- **Wireless CarPlay** — now standard in most new units, eliminates cables
- **Dolby Atmos spatial audio** — Pioneer SPHERA is first aftermarket unit with this (CES 2026)
- **AI voice commands** — Siri + ChatGPT/Grok integration
- **Customizable multi-display dashboards** — instrument cluster + infotainment
- **Native AirPlay video** — stream video when parked (iOS 26+)
- **5V preouts** — important for amplifier/subwoofer expansion
- **iDatalink Maestro** — retains steering wheel controls, factory cameras

### Top Competitor Brands
- Pioneer (SPHERA, DMH-WT8600NEX, DMH-W3050NEX)

- Sony (XAV-9500ES, XAV-AX6000)
