## What You Need for a MK4 CarPlay Upgrade

### 1. Double DIN CarPlay Radio
The MK4 has a double DIN opening, so most 7-inch touchscreen radios will fit. Look for one with:
- Wireless Apple CarPlay
- Android Auto (for passengers with Android phones)
- Bluetooth 5.0+
- Backup camera input
- RCA outputs (if you have an aftermarket amp)

### 2. MK4-Specific Bezel / Dash Kit
This is the most important part for a clean look. The MK4 dash has a unique shape, and a universal bezel will leave gaps. You need a vehicle-specific bezel that:
- Fills the gap around the radio perfectly
- Matches the MK4 dash texture and color
- Holds the radio securely in place

### 3. Wiring Harness Adapter
The MK4 uses a proprietary VW connector. You'll need a harness adapter that:
- Converts the VW plug to standard aftermarket colors
- Supports the factory amplifier (if equipped with the Monsoon system)
- Retains power antenna functionality

### 4. Antenna Adapter (Optional)
If you want to keep FM/AM radio, you'll need a VW antenna adapter. Most MK4s use a "dual FAKRA" connector.

## Installation Tips for the MK4

### Removing the Factory Radio
1. **Remove the trim panel** — The MK4 dash trim pops off with gentle pressure (use plastic pry tools to avoid scratches)
2. **Unscrew the radio** — Four screws hold the factory radio in place
3. **Pull the radio out** — Disconnect the wiring harness and antenna cable

### Installing the New Radio
1. **Connect the wiring harness** — Match colors between the adapter and the new radio
2. **Test before mounting** — Turn on the car and verify CarPlay works before screwing anything in
3. **Install the bezel** — Slide the MK4-specific bezel around the radio
4. **Mount the radio** — Secure with the provided brackets and screws
5. **Route the backup camera** — Run the cable through the trunk hatch and along the door sills

### Common MK4-Specific Issues

**Monsoon Amplifier:** If your MK4 has the Monsoon sound system, you need a special harness that integrates with the factory amp. Without it, you'll get no sound or very quiet sound.

**Steering Wheel Controls:** The MK4 doesn't have factory steering wheel audio controls (on most models), so this isn't a concern. But if you have an aftermarket cruise control with audio buttons, you may need an adapter.

**Dashboard Gaps:** A universal bezel will leave ugly gaps in the MK4 dash. Always use a vehicle-specific bezel for a clean, professional look.
