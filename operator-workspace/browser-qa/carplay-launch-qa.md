# CarPlay Launch QA

## Primary Customer Path

1. Land on page from IG bio or ad.
2. Understand offer within 5 seconds:
   - $109.99 sale price.
   - Regular price $150.
   - Backup camera included.
   - Vehicle-specific kit.
3. Enter year, make, model, and trim/radio details.
4. Submit fitment/order details.
5. Pay through Stripe after fitment confidence is established.
6. Receive clear shipping and replacement-policy expectations.

## Launch Blockers

- Confirm final Stripe product name and $109.99 price.
- Confirm Jotform fields and post-submit message.
- Add real radio, packaging, and install assets.
- Confirm whether payment happens before or after manual fitment review.

## Browser QA Checklist

- Desktop hero does not overflow.
- Mobile hero does not crop important text.
- Main CTA is visible without scrolling on mobile.
- Jotform and Stripe links open in the expected target.
- No customer-facing Amazon/Prime mentions.
- No overpromises: avoid "guaranteed plug and play" and exact install-time claims.
