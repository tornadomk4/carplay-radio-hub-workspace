# Stripe And Jotform Checklist

## Stripe

- Product name: confirm.
- Price: $109.99.
- Customer-facing description: confirm backup camera included and vehicle-specific kit.
- Shipping/tax handling: confirm.
- Success page or post-payment message: confirm.

## Jotform Fields

- Name.
- Email.
- Phone.
- Instagram handle.
- Shipping address.
- Vehicle year.
- Vehicle make.
- Vehicle model.
- Trim, if known.
- Current radio type/photo upload.
- Backup camera confirmation.
- Install confidence: self install, friend/shop, unsure.
- Best contact method.

## Trust Flow

Recommended default: fitment first, then payment link.

Reason: the highest-volume objections are "will it fit?" and "is it plug and play?" A fitment-first flow makes the order feel guided, not random.
